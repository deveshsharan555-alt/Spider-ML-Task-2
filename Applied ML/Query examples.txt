========1.What are the warning signs of a stroke?======

The warning signs of a stroke include sudden numbness or weakness of the face, and other danger signs such as double vision, drowsiness, and nausea or vomiting [1][2][3]. These warning signs may last only a few moments and then disappear, which are sometimes called "mini-strokes" [2][4]. A stroke occurs when the blood supply to part of the brain is suddenly interrupted or when a blood vessel in the brain bursts [5]. If you observe one or more of the signs of a stroke, you should call 911 right away [3]. The warning signs are clues that the brain is not receiving enough oxygen [1].

========2.what are the symptoms of lung cancer========

However, when lung cancer first develops, there may be no symptoms at all [2]. Lung cancer may or may not cause signs or symptoms in its early stages [3]. Common symptoms include coughing, shortness of breath, and chest pain [4]. [1][2][3][4]
